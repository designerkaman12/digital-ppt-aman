The Strategic Shift

What Changed Everything

❌ January - Old Approach

  • 50 posts published in the month
  • High output, low performance
  • Content scattered without strategic focus
  • Minimal differentiation between brands
  • Skip rates of 70–75% — audience not retained
  • Leads stagnant at 1–7 per month

✅ Feb–March - New Approach

  • 25 posts — 50% less volume
  • Each post crafted with intent and strategy
  • Brand-specific content with clear conversion goals
  • Storytelling-led hooks that stop the scroll
  • Skip rate dropped to 45–55%
  • Leads surged to 15–25+ per month
💡 Core Learning: Quality + Strategy consistently outperforms Quantity. Fewer, smarter posts generate exponentially better results across all metrics.
